Why Choose Peace & His Favour

Peace & his Favour School is a Montessori School and at Peace & his Favour School Scholars, we follow the Montessori approach to education, which emphasizes independent learning and hands-on exploration. Our students develop critical thinking skills and a love for learning that lasts a lifetime.

Admissions At Peace & his Favour School, we welcome students of all backgrounds,we are eager to learn in a nurturing and supportive environment. Learn more about our admissions process and how to enroll your child today.


Enhancing Learning At Peace & his Favour School, we offer a range of programs and activities to enhance our students' learning experience. From art and music to sports and community service, there's something for everyone. Explore our programs and activities.
